Titou Granier is a 25 year old cultural event producer, curator and art project manager based in Paris, specializing in contemporary art, luxury activations and international productions.

He started organizing exhibitions during his studies at ICART Paris, earning a degree in Cultural Mediation and Art Market Studies in 2022. That same year, he was offered the opportunity to create the NFT Factory, a 400 square meter digital art gallery facing Centre Pompidou. After a year and a half as Art Manager, he became Director and led the space, curating weekly exhibitions with international artists, producing events for LVMH, Balmain, KPMG, Renault, Binance and more, welcoming up to 5,000 visitors daily. He managed a team of five, oversaw relationships with 128 founders and developed training programs on secure NFT acquisitions.

Since 2024, Titou has worked as a freelance event manager and art curator across Europe and the United States. He coordinated exhibitions for Institut Montaigne at the Musée des Arts et Métiers, organized nine exhibitions in Lisbon for Arab Bank Switzerland and produced private events for Artverse, managing international art shipping logistics to the US and Asia. He has worked on Fashion Week activations, corporate events and pop-up exhibitions in Paris, Geneva, Brussels, New York and Lisbon.

Titou handles everything from budget construction and vendor negotiation to on-site coordination and post-event analysis. He speaks French and English fluently and has built a network across cultural institutions, galleries and luxury brands.

Beyond events and exhibitions, he has directed the artistic production of a photography book, created a full set of playing cards in collaboration with a collector, designed a video game and organized concerts and exhibitions. More projects to come.
